特别强调交通部门的应急管理，本次会议将汇集专家和思想领袖，分享见解、研究成果和最佳实践。

Conference Topics

会议主题

Including but not limited to the following topics:

包括但不限于以下主题：

Resilience Strategies for Transportation Networks

交通网络的韧性策略

Strategies to enhance the resilience of transportation networks involve implementing robust planning, engineering, and operational practices. These strategies aim to mitigate the impact of disruptions, ensure rapid recovery, and maintain the continuous flow of goods and people during adverse events. By integrating resilience measures into transportation planning and management, cities and regions can improve their ability to withstand and recover from various challenges such as natural disasters, accidents, and infrastructure failures.

增强交通网络韧性的策略包括实施强有力的规划、工程和运营实践。这些策略旨在减轻中断的影响，确保快速恢复，并在不利事件中保持货物和人员的持续流动。通过将韧性措施融入交通规划和管理，城市和地区可以提高应对自然灾害、事故和基础设施故障等各种挑战的能力。

Emergency Management in Transportation Systems

交通系统中的应急管理

Emergency management in transportation systems involves preparing for, responding to, and recovering from incidents that disrupt the normal functioning of transportation infrastructure. This includes developing emergency response plans, conducting drills and exercises, coordinating with emergency services, and implementing technologies to enhance real-time situational awareness and streamline response efforts.

交通系统中的应急管理包括为中断交通基础设施正常运作的事件做好准备、响应和恢复。这包括制定应急响应计划，进行演练和练习，与应急服务协调，并实施技术以增强实时态势感知并简化响应工作。

Technological Innovations for Transportation Resilience

增强交通韧性的技术创新

Technological innovations play a crucial role in enhancing the resilience of transportation systems. These innovations include the development and deployment of advanced monitoring and communication systems, predictive analytics, and intelligent transportation systems (ITS) that can detect and respond to potential threats and disruptions in real time. By leveraging technology, transportation agencies can improve their situational awareness, optimize resource allocation, and implement proactive measures to mitigate the impact of disruptions.

技术创新在增强交通系统韧性方面起着至关重要的作用。这些创新包括开发和部署先进的监控和通信系统、预测分析和智能交通系统（ITS），能够实时检测和响应潜在威胁和中断。通过利用技术，交通管理机构可以提高态势感知能力，优化资源配置，并采取主动措施以减轻中断的影响。

Transportation System Vulnerability and Risk Assessment

交通系统脆弱性和风险评估

Transportation system vulnerability and risk assessment involve identifying and analyzing potential threats and hazards that could impact the functioning of transportation networks. This includes evaluating the likelihood and consequences of various disruptive events, such as natural disasters, accidents, cyber-attacks, and infrastructure failures. By conducting vulnerability and risk assessments, transportation agencies can prioritize resources, implement mitigation measures, and enhance the resilience of their networks to better withstand and recover from adverse events.

交通系统脆弱性和风险评估包括识别和分析可能影响交通网络运行的潜在威胁和危害。这包括评估各种破坏性事件（如自然灾害、事故、网络攻击和基础设施故障）的可能性和后果。通过进行脆弱性和风险评估，交通管理机构可以优先分配资源，实施缓解措施，并增强网络的韧性，以更好地承受和恢复不利事件。

Traffic Incident and Crisis Management

交通事故和危机管理

Traffic incident and crisis management is a comprehensive approach to handling transportation-related incidents and emergencies. It encompasses the prevention, preparedness, response, and recovery phases of an incident, aiming to minimize casualties, property damage, and disruption while restoring normal transportation operations. Effective traffic incident and crisis management involve coordinating multiple stakeholders, including first responders, traffic management agencies, law enforcement, and the public.

交通事故和危机管理是处理交通相关事故和紧急情况的综合方法。它包括事故的预防、准备、响应和恢复阶段，旨在尽量减少伤亡、财产损失和中断，同时恢复正常的交通运营。有效的交通事故和危机管理涉及协调多个利益相关者，包括急救人员、交通管理机构、执法部门和公众。

Urban Planning and Transportation Resilience

城市规划与交通韧性

Urban planning and transportation resilience is an integrated and strategic approach to designing and developing urban infrastructure to maintain operational continuity and quickly recover in the face of disruptive events such as natural disasters, technological failures, or human-made disruptions. By combining urban planning principles and transportation resilience strategies, cities can enhance the robustness, responsiveness, and adaptability of their transportation networks, ensuring safe and sustainable travel options for residents while minimizing economic and social disruptions.

城市规划和交通韧性是综合和战略性的方法，用于设计和开发城市基础设施，以在自然灾害、技术故障或人为干扰等破坏性事件面前保持运营连续性并迅速恢复。通过结合城市规划原则和交通韧性策略，城市可以增强其交通网络的坚韧性、响应能力和适应性，确保居民的安全和可持续的出行选择，同时最大限度地减少经济和社会中断。
